3 August 2020

2020-21 Season Update

Due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, all fall 2020 Golden Bear Ice Hockey activities have been put on hold. All tryouts, games, and practices have been postponed until further notice.

Cal Ice Hockey focuses to ensure the safety of every player, coach, staff member, and fan during this extremely difficult time.

Nevertheless, a Spring 2021 return for Cal Ice Hockey and the rest of the PAC-8 could be possible in the event of improvements in the status of the ongoing pandemic. We hope to work towards this goal in the coming months and will provide an update when we have a clearer picture on these developments.

As always, Cal Ice Hockey is extremely grateful for its loyal fans and supporters, and would like to get back on the ice for them as soon as it is safe to do so.

In the meantime, fans looking to catch up on Golden Bear Hockey can watch many of our 2019-2020 Regular Season games here.

Stay Safe and Go Bears!
